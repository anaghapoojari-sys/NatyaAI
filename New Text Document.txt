Project Title: Real-Time Multimodal Posture Correction, Rhythm Alignment, and Semantic Translation System for Bharatanatyam Dance.

Objective: To develop an intelligent, real-time computer vision and audio processing system that evaluates a Bharatanatyam dancer's physical geometry (Nritya), matches their movements to rhythmic audio patterns (Nattuvangam/Sollukattu), and translates the expressive hand gestures and facial features (Abhinaya) into text-based semantic meanings.
